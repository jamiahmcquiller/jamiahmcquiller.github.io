Empowering Futures: A Cybersecurity Journey of Resilience and Inclusivity

In the pursuit of my academic and career aspirations, I seamlessly weave my passion for Cybersecurity with a dedication to influence my community positively. As a committed single mother, my enduring objective is to emerge as a notable figure in cybersecurity, employing ethical hacking to fortify digital landscapes and champion inclusivity. Beyond technical proficiency, my commitment is a response to technology's profound impact on communities. As a black female navigating a male-dominated field, I aspire to inspire diverse voices, nurturing a welcoming and empowered cybersecurity environment. The delicate balance of parenthood enhances my resilience, propelling me to secure our digital future.

Throughout my academic journey, I've excelled, boasting a high GPA and impactful projects. As a Dean's Think Tank member, I strategically enhanced the student experience. Serving as Girls Who Code Treasurer, I implemented financial strategies, resulting in a notable improvement. In the Undergrad Research Opportunity Program (UROP) and UMake The Change, I conducted profound research, showcasing project management, organizational skills, and hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies. Recognition in the President's List and the National Society of Leadership and Success underscores my dedication to academic excellence, serving as examples for my daughter, emphasizing active participation, community contribution, and the link between hard work and success.

As a research assistant at UM-Flint's Computational Medicine and Biomechanics Lab, pivotal contributions were made in developing 'Patient-Specific VR' software, enhancing cardiovascular disease visualization and analysis. Engaging in computational medicine and machine learning, I honed skills in applying numerical techniques and interdisciplinary work, such as stimulating heart contractions and developing social skills in robots. Proposing a novel augmentation for safe discussions on ethnic and racial issues showcases my innovative research design and commitment to addressing societal challenges. These skills position me as a well-rounded professional applicable to various fields, highlighting my dedication to continuous learning and growth.

Education, a cornerstone of my growth, instills not just knowledge but resilience, adaptability, and a sense of social responsibility. This transformative impact is evident in challenging projects during my research role. Balancing academic rigor with single motherhood marked a defining chapter, showcasing deepened technical proficiency, resilience, and creative problem-solving. This journey affirms that being a single mother is integral to my identity, propelling me forward with unwavering determination.

In conclusion, my commitment is to bring a unique perspective to the College of Innovation and Technology at the University of Michigan-Flint. Through initiatives like UROP and UMake The Change, I'll apply my skills in innovative problem-solving, inclusion, and leadership, tangibly contributing to academic excellence and community impact. Dedicated to initiatives aligning with my passion for breaking barriers, my commitment resonates with the university's mission of engaged citizenship and fostering diverse voices. Eager to contribute to the College of Innovation and Technology, I aim to set an example for my daughter and others, illustrating how determination and active contribution bring dreams to life at the University of Michigan.
