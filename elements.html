Jamiah McQuiller

I am a junior at the University of Michigan, currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity with a Minor in Computer Science. Alongside my academic journey, I've been proactive in expanding my skill set and practical experience. I hold the certification of a Certified Associate Cloud Engineer in Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and have gained invaluable hands-on experience through an IT Co-op position at Nexteer Automotive. Recently, my passion for problem-solving led me to complete the Google Career Certificate program in Google Cloud. This program equipped me with a robust foundation in Cloud Infrastructure, networking, and systems administration. Leveraging this knowledge, I've successfully deployed applications, monitored operations, and managed enterprise solutions using the Google Cloud Console, culminating in earning the Associate Cloud Engineer certification. Moreover, my academic endeavors have been complemented by practical achievements. I am proud to have placed second at the UMake The Change and Zillion Solutions competition for my 4D Flow Cardiac MRI Data Visualization using Virtual Reality (VR) Technology. This experience not only allowed me to showcase my technical abilities but also highlighted my creativity and innovative thinking. Additionally, my educational background spans coursework at both Colorado Technical University and the University of Michigan, covering a wide array of subjects including Python and Java programming, Windows and Linux operating systems, and Information Technology. With a solid foundation in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, and Cloud Infrastructure, coupled with practical experiences and achievements, I am eager to bring significant value to your team.
